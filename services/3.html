What are Laboratory Services?

Laboratory services involve various tests conducted on samples like blood, urine, or tissue to help diagnose diseases, monitor health, and guide treatment decisions. Reliable lab results are crucial for effective medical care.

Common Lab Tests Offered

  • Blood sugar (glucose) testing
  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Liver and kidney function tests
  • Cholesterol and lipid profile
  • HIV, malaria, and hepatitis screening
  • Urinalysis and stool tests

Why Lab Testing Matters

Timely laboratory diagnostics help detect diseases at an early stage, track treatment progress, and tailor medical approaches to individual patients. It improves accuracy in healthcare decisions.
