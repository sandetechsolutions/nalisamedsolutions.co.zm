GENERAL CONSULTATION

First step to comprehensive healthcare

What We Offer

General consultations are your first point of contact with a healthcare professional. They help diagnose health issues, provide medical advice, and direct you to appropriate specialists if needed.

When to Book a Consultation

  • Unexplained symptoms
  • General illness or fatigue
  • Health checkups & clearances
  • Chronic disease follow-up
  • Medication reviews

Our Approach

Our doctors take time to listen, assess, and provide tailored guidance. Early medical advice often prevents escalation of health conditions.

Note: Walk-ins and scheduled appointments are both welcome.
