Why Eye Care is Important

The eye clinic focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of vision problems and eye diseases. Regular eye exams are important for detecting issues like glaucoma, cataracts, and refractive errors early.

Common Vision Concerns

  • Blurry or double vision
  • Eye strain or headaches
  • Red, itchy, or painful eyes
  • Night blindness or light sensitivity
  • Glaucoma, cataracts, or macular degeneration
  • Dry eye syndrome

Our Eye Clinic Services

We offer full eye evaluations, prescription glasses, treatment of infections and chronic conditions, and surgical referrals when necessary. Your vision is our priority.
