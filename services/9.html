Restoring movement and improving quality of life

What is Physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy helps restore movement and function when someone is affected by injury, illness, or disability. It also promotes long-term health and wellness through personalized rehabilitation.

Common Treatments

  • Back and neck pain therapy
  • Post-surgery rehabilitation
  • Stroke and paralysis support
  • Sports injuries recovery
  • Joint mobilization and pain relief

Why Physiotherapy?

Timely physiotherapy improves mobility, reduces pain, and prevents further injury. It enhances independence and overall health outcomes across all age groups.

Note: We offer both inpatient and outpatient physiotherapy sessions.
