What is Endocrinology & Diabetes?

Endocrinology is the branch of medicine concerned with the endocrine system, its diseases, and its specific secretions called hormones. Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy, commonly caused by issues in insulin production or function.

Common Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst and hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss or gain
  • Fatigue and blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores or frequent infections
  • Hormonal imbalance symptoms (e.g., irregular periods, hair loss)

Why Seek Medical Attention?

Untreated diabetes or hormonal imbalance can lead to severe health complications, including heart disease, kidney failure, nerve damage, infertility, and vision loss. Early diagnosis and continuous treatment are essential to prevent or delay these complications and ensure better quality of life.
