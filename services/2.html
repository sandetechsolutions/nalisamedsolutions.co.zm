What is Dental Care?

Dental care involves the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the teeth, gums, and mouth. Good oral health is vital to overall wellbeing, and regular checkups ensure early detection and prevention.

Common Dental Concerns

  • Toothache or sensitivity
  • Cavities or tooth decay
  • Bleeding or swollen gums
  • Bad breath (halitosis)
  • Loose or missing teeth
  • Misaligned teeth or bite issues

Why Visit a Dentist?

Regular dental visits help prevent oral diseases, maintain a healthy smile, and address issues early before they become complicated. Preventive care reduces the risk of tooth loss and expensive procedures.
