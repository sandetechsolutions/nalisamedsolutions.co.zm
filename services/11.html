What is Ultrasound?

Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves to capture live images inside the body. It helps visualize organs, tissues, and fetal development safely without radiation.

Types of Ultrasound

  • Obstetric (pregnancy monitoring)
  • Abdominal (liver, kidneys, bladder)
  • Pelvic (uterus, ovaries, prostate)
  • Breast, thyroid, and soft tissue scans
  • Vascular and Doppler studies

Why Ultrasound?

Ultrasound is quick, safe, and highly effective in detecting abnormalities. It aids in early diagnosis, safe monitoring during pregnancy, and guiding minor procedures.
