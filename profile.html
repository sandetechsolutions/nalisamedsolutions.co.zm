About

Dr. David Lubasi Nalisa is an Endocrinologist and Researcher affiliated with The First Affiliated Hospital of Henan University of Science and Technology in Luoyang, China. His groundbreaking research focuses on Stem Cell Therapy, Diabetic Foot Ulcers (DFU), and Obesity, aiming to transform therapeutic approaches in endocrinology and regenerative medicine.

Key Research Areas

  • Stem Cell Therapy for Diabetic Foot Ulcers: Demonstrated 86.41% success rate in Wagner-II lesions using mesenchymal stem cell application (SCA), significantly reducing the risk of amputation.
  • Mazdutide for Weight Loss: Investigated dual agonist Mazdutide for diabetes patients, showing reductions in body weight, blood pressure, and cholesterol.
  • Adipose-Derived Stem Cells: Exploring wound healing and tissue repair mechanisms using adipose-derived stem cells.
  • Extracellular Vesicles: Studying vesicles from adipose mesenchymal stem cells in diabetic wound healing.
  • Cell-Based Therapy: Developing innovative therapies for non-healing diabetic ulcers using allogeneic adipose-derived stem cells.
